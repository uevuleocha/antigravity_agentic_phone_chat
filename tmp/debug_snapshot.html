💡 Will this fix work?

Yes, this is the root cause.

In CSS, size-contained elements (container-type: size) completely isolate layout sizing from their children. When placed inside a height: auto parent, they collapse to 0px because their size cannot be determined from the inside out. Since the inner elements use 100cqh (100% of the collapsed container height) and overflow-hidden, they clip everything out of view. Disabling container-type: size and replacing min-height: calc(100cqh - 50px) with min-height: auto client-side allows the browser to calculate layout sizes naturally on the phone.
